Here To Help

We all know support is likely out there for our struggles, but sometimes we need it most from the people that matter to us and are too worried about the outcomes to ask for it.

Here at Reach Out you can use this platform to start those difficult conversations anonymously. Have a read of our users testimonies to see how this service has helped better their lives and how you can do the same for yourself.

Anna

I was scared from all the old horror stories of kids being shunned or abandoned by their friends and family for their sexuality. I used Reach Out to tell my friends that 'someone they knew' was struggling to talk about it. After receieving a number of encouraging messages it gave me the confidence to come out in person and shortly after told my parents! I'm finally feeling more like the real me.

Leeroy

For a long time I felt alone in my interests. I don't enjoy football and felt left out at school because of it. I used Reach Out to ask if any of the people I knew would be interested in hanging out to play boardgames. I now have a close circle of friends and we meet up regularly to play Dungeons and Dragons!

Kate

After my husband passed away I became incredibly reclusive and scared I was a burden on the people around me. When I found this website I contacted the people I knew to ask for help. I was shocked at first that they accepted and wanted me to reach out to them for help despite not fully knowing who I was. My friendships have become deeper, more meaningful and I have since found the confidence in myself to try dating again.

Don't Hesitate

The longer you take to open up, the longer you spend not living your best life. There's no reason to take on these challenges alone. There are people out there willing to help you.

You shouldn't feel alone. Remember.

You are cared for: we care about you, as do support services and so should friends and family (or your created family). They are already out there. Reach out and let them in.

You are worth it. You can tell yourself.

You deserve quality relationships: make building quality relationships a priority over a quantity of relationships. The bonds you have with people can help to combat loneliness. Take time to strengthen your relationships. Tell yourself you deserve this.

Everyone is special. Including you.

You are unique: not following the crowd doesn't make you alone, it makes you unique. We love who you are. There is nothing to be ashamed of.

There are always others. Waiting to meet you.

You can find your community: by joining a group whether based on a hobby, religion, spirituality or an interest can help you 'find your tribe'. There are people out there waiting to connect with you. Who are just as scared to make the first move.

Our Promise To You

We want you to know that all of your information here is kept private. It will be up to you to reveal your identity, should you choose to, to the people you reach out to. We also understand not everyone will get the response they are hoping for and if the supports that we have listed don't cover your needs, please reach out to us and we will try to help guide you however we can.
