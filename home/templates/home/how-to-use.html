How to Use Reach Out

Below you'll find a guide that will lead you step by step on how to go about preparing a message to the people you want to contact. The only thing you will need is their email address.

Michael

After my divorce I ended up living alone in an apartment closer to work. I had lost most of my friends because of what had happened. I reached out through building managment to the other residents living in my apartment block and asking for help. I couldn't tell you, at the time, how surprised I was by the kindness of strangers. I have made many new friends and have been working hard to become better than the man I was before.

Zack

Whilst away at university I told my parents that someone close to them (I) was planning on transitioning. My father didn't respond and my mother was very dismissive and against the idea of someone she knew making such changes. But, with the support services I found through this site I have found the words and strength to speak about my feelings more confidently. Despite not getting the response I had hoped for it has pushed me in the right direction to start making the changes I need. I am much happier with who I am and have since come out about it to my parents before trying to go for surgery. With the conversations we had online, I think it has given them enough time to reflect on their thoughts of what they might have sensed was coming.

Maria

As a single mother I had struggled with alcohol abuse for a while. I used this platform to reach out to my friends to say that I desperately needed help from people I could trust and that could keep the addiction I needed help fighting a secret. I have recieved a huge ammount of support, I have friends who help look after my child in the evenings when I go to my AA meetings, I have been given a weath of information to help regain control my finances and I am making changes to better the lives of me and my child.

The first thing you will need to do is register an account with us. This is just for security purposes, none of the details you submit will be shared.

Once you've verified your account, you can go to the reach out page where you will be given options on how to craft your message.

We've created a number of template messages to help you with various topics of conversation.

If the none of the templates suit your needs, you can select to craft your own message.

After sending your message, the recipients that you listed will recieve an email inviting them to chat. If they accept, they'll be brought to this website where you can send each other messages through the website. Your identity will remain hidden unless you choose to tell them.

If you're unable to get the response or help that you were hoping for, don't be disheartened. There are plenty of professional services out there who want to help you with your struggles and we're proud of you for taking this difficult step.
